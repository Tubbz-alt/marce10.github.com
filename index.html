The advantage of R over most common sound analysis software is its higher flexibility, which allows the implementation of custom made analyses that better fit the research questions and the characteristics of the vocalizations. Some analyses, however, require quite elaborated R routines. I will share my acoustic analysis rountines here. Note that many of the tools I have developed have been made available in the package warbleR

. I will also post R scripts to detail the usage of new addtions to the package.
